
# User-Interface-and-User-experience


## User Interface (UI):
User Interface refers to the visual elements through which users interact with a product or service — such as buttons, icons, menus, colors, and layout. It focuses on the look and feel of a digital product.


## User Experience (UX):
User Experience is the overall experience a user has when interacting with a product or service. It includes usability, accessibility, ease of navigation, and the emotional response a user feels. UX is about solving problems and creating meaningful experiences.

## 🌸 Overview

This project showcases a **Valentine's Day brand advertisement** designed for **Adidas**, centered around their **Grand Court Base Tennis Shoes** – with a soft **pink color palette** that captures warmth, romance, and style.

🔗 The concept is aimed at:
- **Fashion-forward women** who love comfort & style.
- **Men** looking to gift the perfect pair of sneakers this Valentine’s Day.

---

## 🎨 Theme & Style

> 💗 **Color Theme**: Shades of pink to reflect femininity, love, and freshness  
> ✨ **Tone**: Playful, elegant, and romantic  
> 🧼 **Design Aesthetic**: Clean, minimal, and modern with bold text callouts  
> 🫶 **Emotional Hook**: “Perfect for HER. Picked by HIM.”

The entire layout is **frame-based**, layered with animation and soft gradients to elevate the emotional appeal.

## 🧪 Creative Process

### 🖼️ 1. Moodboarding
Curated visuals from **Pinterest** that matched the vibe — romantic, sporty, soft, feminine.

### 🧱 2. Frame-by-Frame Layout
Built out each section using **Adobe Express frames**, layering text, images, and animations.

### 💬 3. Typography & Copywriting
Used **contrasting fonts** to guide attention and styled the ad copy using online quote inspiration to sound emotionally resonant.

### 💾 4. Assembly & Polish
Finalized the look by balancing visual weight, applying a soft animation touch, and making the ad **social-media friendly**.
